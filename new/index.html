Hello hello! I am faculty of Design at the department of design of IIT Gandhinagar. I work in game design, game studies, interaction design, and technology studies.

My doctoral research theorises fun in abstract strategy table-top games. It establishes fun as the fundamental aesthetic of games. It creates an event ontology, identifies conditions in which these events occur, and places player intents at the center of aesthetic analysis of gameplay.
